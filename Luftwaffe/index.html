Luftwaffe Aces

This is a single player arcade style game of aerial combat in World War II. It is a scrolling shooter type game, inspired by the CapCom games "1942" and "Midway 1943". You will be flying against a large number of enemy aircraft, with your goal being to break records held by actual Luftwaffe (German Air Force) pilots of the war. Can you match Erich Hartmann's incredible feat of shooting down 352 allied planes?
